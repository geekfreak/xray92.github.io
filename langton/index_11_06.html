Join Us For Langton's Gala Dinner, Preview Party, and 22nd Annual Art Auction, Thursday November 2

A spectacular party on the night includes a Gala Dinner with Chez Panisse head chef David Tanis and Jerome Waag, who prepare an unparalleled feast and performance dining event.

The Auction Preview Party upstairs features music and performances, and a not-to-be-missed San Francisco night out with artists, collectors, benefactors, and other patrons.

And, Langton’s exclusive Live Auction features nearly 40 works by new, emerging, and established artists of regional, national, and international talent.

Collecting is a compulsion we encourage you to indulge.
