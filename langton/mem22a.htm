Donate
 

In-Kind Donations

You can support Langton through in-kind gifts of goods or services, such as computer hardware or office equipment. You'll receive membership benefits, and your contribution is tax-deductible to the full extent of the law.

Langton is looking for a fire-proof lock box, office phone system, color laser printer, Apple computers, and donations of printing services. If you are interested in donating any of these items to Langton, please contact the gallery at nla@newlangtonarts.org or call 415 626 5416.

Auction Sponsorships

Langton's Annual Auction is a unique opportunity for companies and corporations to support Langton through cash and in-kind donations. You or your company receive repeated exposure to as many as 5,000 artists, art collectors, business leaders, educators, and visitors who reflect the richness and complexity of the region's communities. All proceeds benefit New Langton Arts and its artistic programs.

Donate online with PayPal.


Planned Giving

Making a planned gift, such as including a bequest to Langton in your will or naming Langton as the beneficiary of a charitable remainder trust or retirement fund, ensures that Langton and its programs will develop and flourish in the years to come.

If you would like more information about planned gifts, please contact us at nla@newlangtonarts.org or call 415.626.5416.

Cash and in-kind contributions are tax-deductible to the full extent of the law.
