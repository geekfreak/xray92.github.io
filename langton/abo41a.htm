Hours and Location
 


New Langton Arts
1246 Folsom Street (between 8th and 9th streets)
San Francisco, CA 94103-3817
415 626 5416


Gallery Hours and Tours
Tuesday - Saturday, 12 -6 pm.
Gallery admission is free.

Tours of Langton exhibitions, conducted by staff or presenting artists, are available by appointment. Just call and let us know when you are coming.


Box Office Hours and Ticket Information
Tuesday - Saturday, 12 - 6 pm and 1 hour before performances.

Call 415 626 5416 to reserve tickets by phone.
Advance reservations are recommended. Reservations and complimentary tickets are held until 15 minutes before start time.

Ticket purchases may be made by phone with Visa or Mastercard by phone. Please note that a $1/ticket service charge applies for all credit card purchases.

Discounted Tickets
  • Langton members receive 25% off all theater tickets. Why not take advantage of the offer? Join today.
  • Groups of 10 or more receive 1/2 off the general admission price for theater events. Group tickets are available by reservation only.
  • Langton's Caregivers Program has been offering nurses, teachers, child care workers, volunteers, social workers, and civil servants complimentary admission to theater events since 1996. Please call for a reservation and bring your work ID.


Directions to Langton:

From the East Bay: Take the Bay Bridge to San Francisco (80 West). Take the Ninth Street Exit, then go straight onto Harrison. Go one block and turn right at Ninth Street. Go one block and turn right at Folsom Street. Langton is located half a block down Folsom on the left side.

From the South Bay: Take Highway 101 North to San Francisco. Take the Ninth Street Exit. Veer to the left onto Ninth Street (do not make the sharp left turn as you exit the highway). Go two blocks and turn right on Folsom Street. Langton is located half a block down Folsom on the left side.

From the North Bay: Take the Golden Gate Bridge to San Francisco (101 South). Follow 101 South along Lombard and Van Ness. Turn left on Fell Street. Follow Fell for one block, then cross Market and go straight on Tenth Street. Go three blocks and turn left on Folsom Street. Langton is located one and a half blocks down Folsom on the left side.

Public Transportation
Take MUNI or BART to Civic Center. Walk south on 8th Street past Mission and Howard and turn right on Folsom.
