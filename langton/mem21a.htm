Become a Member
 

 

Join Langton today. Your contribution supports art at the source.

As a member of New Langton Arts you have a direct impact on the artworks presented by ensuring that the artists have the funds needed to make their work and the space to exhibit them to the public.

Since 1975 New Langton Arts has been supporting contemporary art and artists in the creation of new works through exhibitions, performances, lectures, publications, and grants -- works that challenge their makers and their audiences. We greatly appreciate all levels of support, and every gift makes a difference.

How to Join

Langton accepts cash, checks, MasterCard, and Visa.
If you have any questions, please contact membership at nla@newlangtonarts.org or call us at 415.626.5416

or join online with PayPal.

• Stock Gifts

Langton gladly accepts gifts of stock and securities. For account numbers and transfer information, email nla@newlangtonarts.org or call 415.626.5416.

• Corporate Matching Gifts

You can increase the value of your contribution to Langton if your employer has a matching gift program. Please contact your company's human resources or community relations department to determine whether your gift to Langton can be matched by your employer.
